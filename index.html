Who are we?

We are Heeren 6, a hockey team at the student hockey club DHC in Enschede. This is a team with extraordinary hockey skills, and members that hit the bar of the hockey club, and try to hit the hockey ball. All of us study at the University of Twente and the Saxion, and participate in a wide range of different studies at different study levels. We are a team where you can join our hockey trainings and/or our hockey games. Contact us and join our game!
